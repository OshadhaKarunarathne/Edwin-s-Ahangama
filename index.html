Welcome to Edwin’s Ahangama

A calm boutique stay on Sri Lanka’s southern coast

Tucked away in the heart of Ahangama, Edwin’s Ahangama is a newly opened private villa designed for travelers who appreciate simplicity, comfort, and a peaceful atmosphere.

Surrounded by nature and just minutes from the beach, the villa offers a refined yet relaxed stay ideal for slow travel, beach escapes, and longer stays. It is a family-owned villa created with the idea that true comfort comes from calm surroundings, thoughtful design, and genuine hospitality.

Highlights

  • Private boutique villa with three bedrooms
  • Calm, elegant interiors with natural light
  • Close to beaches, surf spots, and cafés
  • Ideal for couples, families, and mindful stays
